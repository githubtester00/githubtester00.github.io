WHAT IS CREATECH?

Createch is the Tinkering Club of Ryan International School, Vasant Kunj. It’s a place for all kinds of Technology enthusiasts – be it Design, Development, Audio, Video or Robotics – as well as for beginners wishing to enhance their knowledge in any of these fields.We conduct sessions in various tech categories, represent the school in tinkering and technology-related competitions/events, conduct intra-competitions and projects for our members, organize our annual inter-school event and much more!

WHO CAN JOIN THE CLUB?

Learners, tech-enthusiasts, people wishing to take sessions in any tech-related field, and people wishing to take part in competitions, projects and help during our events.The club is open to students from all classes in Ryan Vasant Kunj, provided they have the pre-requisite knowledge for the sessions that they wish to take.

Do you have an annual inter-school event?

Yes. The first edition of our event was held on 19 November, 2019. Stay tuned for the fourth edition this year – more details coming soon.
